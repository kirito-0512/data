1941,
1941 (MCMXLI) was a common year starting on Wednesday of the Gregorian calendar, the 1941st year of the Common Era (CE) and Anno Domini (AD) designations, the 941st  year of the 2nd millennium, the 41st  year of the 20th century, and the  2nd   year of the 1940s decade. Below, the events of World War II have the "WWII" prefix.
