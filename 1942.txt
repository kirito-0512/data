1942 (MCMXLII) was a common year starting on Thursday of the Gregorian calendar, the 1942nd year of the Common Era (CE) and Anno Domini (AD) designations, the 942nd  year of the 2nd millennium, the 42nd  year of the 20th century, and the  3rd   year of the 1940s decade. Below, the events of World War II have the "WWII" prefix.
